The Cell Cycle

By Will Hamon and Dylan Glantz

Interphase
The cell spends 90% of its life in this phase. This phase is split up into 3 subphases; Gap1, Synthesis, Gap2. G1 is the first phase, in it the cell grows and carries out normal cell functions. S phase is the second phase, in it the cell copies and replicates its DNA. In the last phase, G2, the cell continues to grow and prepares for cell division.
